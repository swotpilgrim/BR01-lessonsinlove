## ROOT CAUSE ANALYSIS

I made chapters 10-12 too romantic too fast, which broke the story progression:

1. **Chapter 10-11:** I had them become a full couple with public kissing
2. **Chapter 12:** I jumped to father's visit and bribe
3. **Chapter 13:** I created a party scene and "I love you" confession
4. **Chapter 14:** I had them commit officially instead of almost-kiss tension
5. **Chapter 15:** Scene mostly right but context wrong (they're already together)

The outlines had a SLOW BURN:
- Ch 10: First connection, shift beyond tutoring
- Ch 11: Growing attraction but NO MOVE made yet
- Ch 12: Emma warns, Rachel pulls back
- Ch 13: Blake FAILS to defend Rachel (key turning point)
- Ch 14: Almost-kiss that doesn't happen (tension builds)
- Ch 15: Blake finally stands up for her publicly (turning point)

I collapsed this arc and invented my own where they get together faster with different drama beats.
